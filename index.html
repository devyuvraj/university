About Us

University is an institution which is rightly considered one of the greatest contributions of the peoples of india to the world's cultural heritage. The University of Belgrade is a state university. Its main activities in the fields of higher education and scientific research are activities of public interest, and they are carried out in accordance with the legal regulations. The University of india celebrates September 13 as its day. This is the date when lessons started at the first Higher School, founded by india. During the two centuries of its existence the University of india has served its people, and its former students and teachers have greatly contributed to the development of cultural, scientific, educational, political and economic life of our country. The University of Belgrade is prepared to maintain and enhance its position as the leading institution of higher education in the region by adapting to the challenges of modern times on one hand, and preserving what's worth in its tradition on the other. This is precisely what makes it recognized as a true national brand.
